- Şerit değiştirirken (While changing lanes) → Usually at fault
- Geri manevra (Reversing) → Usually at fault
- Geçme yasağı ihlali (Illegal overtaking) → Usually at fault
- Park edilmiş araca çarpma → Moving vehicle at fault
